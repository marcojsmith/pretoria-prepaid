# Initial Concept

A web application for tracking and calculating prepaid electricity purchases in Pretoria, South Africa, using Convex for the backend and Clerk for authentication.

# Product Definition: Pretoria Prepaid

## Overview

Pretoria Prepaid is a specialized web application designed to help residents of Pretoria, South Africa, effectively manage and optimize their prepaid electricity spending. By leveraging the local tiered tariff system, the app provides users with precise cost calculations, consumption tracking, and historical insights. As a Progressive Web App (PWA), it remains fully functional during power outages and intermittent connectivity, ensuring users can always track their utility spending.

## Project Goals

- **Spending Optimization:** Help users understand and minimize their electricity costs by strategically timing purchases within the tiered tariff structure.
- **Purchase Tracking:** Provide a seamless interface for logging and reviewing all prepaid electricity purchases over time.
- **Cost Prediction:** Enable users to estimate future electricity costs and unit allocations based on their current consumption patterns and South Africa's tiered pricing models.

## Target Audience

- **Pretoria Residents:** Specifically individuals and households using the City of Tshwane's prepaid electricity meters who want better visibility into their utility expenses.

## Core Features

- **Dashboard & Analytics:** A central hub for visualizing monthly spending trends, consumption patterns, and progress through electricity tiers.
- **Purchase Calculator:** A tool that allows users to input a Rand amount and see exactly how many units (kWh) they will receive after accounting for tiers and service fees.
- **History & Export:** A comprehensive log of all past purchases with the ability to export data for personal record-keeping or budget analysis.
- **Offline First:** Fully functional offline mode for calculating and viewing history, with background synchronization for data entry when reconnected.

## Product Vision

- **Local Utility Tool:** To become the indispensable local utility management companion for every household in Pretoria, providing data-driven financial insights into household energy management.
