# Product Guidelines: Pretoria Prepaid

## Prose Style

- **Innovative & Enthusiastic:** The application's voice should be upbeat and modern, positioning itself as an innovative tool for local utility management.
- **Supportive & Encouraging:** When users reach optimization milestones or discover savings, the tone should celebrate these small wins.

## Branding & UI

- **Clean & Data-First:** The user interface should be minimalist, ensuring that critical data like current units and tier progress remains the primary focus.
- **Fast & Responsive:** The UI must feel instantaneous, with smooth transitions and a modern single-page application (SPA) architecture that reflects its cutting-edge design.

## User Experience (UX) Principles

- **Real-time Feedback:** Leverage Convex's real-time capabilities to provide immediate updates across the dashboard and calculator, ensuring the app always feels "alive."
- **Visual Simplicity:** Complex electricity tariff structures should be distilled into intuitive, easy-to-read charts, progress bars, and simple labels.
- **Efficiency-focused UX:** The purchase logging and calculation flows must be optimized for speed, allowing users to enter and process data with minimal friction.

## Accessibility

- **Mobile-First Accessibility:** Given the likelihood of on-the-go usage, the interface must prioritize large, easy-to-tap targets and a layout that is fully functional on mobile devices while maintaining standard web accessibility.
- **High-Contrast Data:** Important numbers and status indicators should be clearly visible in various lighting conditions, ensuring readability for all users.
